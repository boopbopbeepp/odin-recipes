Potato Salad

Description

This potato salad is quick to make for a delicious summer side dish with old-fashioned flavor. Make it the day before you plan to serve it for the best flavor. Sure to be a hit at any BBQ or potluck!

Why You'll Love It

  • This recipe calls for red potatoes, tangy pickles, and hard-cooked eggs for classic summer flavor.
  • Home cooks love the easy-to-follow steps and the flexibility of the ingredients.
  • It's a perfect side dish for barbecues, picnics, and family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds red potatoes, chopped
  • 3 cups mayonnaise
  • 2 cups finely chopped pickles
  • 5 large hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • ½ cup chopped red onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 3 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper or to taste

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 10 minutes. Drain. Return potatoes to the empty pot to dry and cool.
  3. Stir mayonnaise, pickles, hard-cooked eggs, red onion, celery, mustard, and cider vinegar together in a large bowl; season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Fold in cooled potatoes until well combined.
  5. For best results, store potato salad in the refrigerator until chilled or overnight before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
